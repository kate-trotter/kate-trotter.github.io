Boudoir

Together we'll design a custom session to showcase your features and personality. Sessions are usually about 2 hours, with time beforehand for our hair and makeup artist to glam you up!

Pets

These are typically casual, in-home lifestyle sessions that capture memories of your pets at this specific stage of your life. Whether it's the cat who always knew how to comfort you after a bad test or the dog who is the true firstborn of the family. There is never a bad time to capture the love and bond shared between you. Together we'll create tangible memories that you can treasure long after the kids are grown and the pet has passed. Pets, especially cats, are typically most comfortable in their home environment, and that allows their personality to shine through! I love learning the quirks of each pet and capturing the love they have for you, even though your cat is likely a bit stoic about it.

Motherhood

Whether you are newly expecting, or your children have kids of their own now, I am here to capture moments in the journey that is motherhood. These sessions are also typically more casual, though locations vary, and about remembering the small intimate moments that define each stage. So your toddlers are obsessed with helping you in the kitchen? I got you. So you want one smiling picture from the teenage angst years? I got you. Want generation photos your family will treasure? I got you.
