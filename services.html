Step 1: Connect

It all begins with an initial consultation designed to establish a personal connection. During this stage, we'll take the time to understand your desires and vision. We'll discuss the type of session you're interested in, explore potential locations, and brainstorm styling options. Our aim is to get to know you and create a custom session and quote that is specifically tailored to you.

Step 2: Create an Atmosphere of Trust

Trusting someone with your portrait is a big deal, and I'm here to cultivate that with you. Your comfort is my priority, and I want you to feel empowered and beautiful throughout the entire experience. I have worked with diverse individuals, respecting and celebrating all body types, backgrounds, and comfort levels. Our collaboration is key, and we'll create mood boards, discuss styling choices, and have open lines of communication to ensure that your portraits are a true reflection of your unique personality and desires.

Step 3: Image Selection and Retouching

I will reach out to you to schedule an ordering appointment, where we can share the excitement of selecting your images. Whether we meet in person or connect over Zoom, this will be a special moment to appreciate the artistry we've created together. We'll discuss various options for prints, albums, paintings, and wall art, ensuring that your final products are customized to your preferences. Rest assured, your images will always be handled securely and will never be shared without your permission.

Step 4: The Final Reveal

The final reveal marks the culmination of your journey, and I'm dedicated to making it a meaningful experience for you!
