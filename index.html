Common Myths About Boudoir:

Boudoir is raunchy

Actually, it is a body empowerment experience. These intimate portraits focus on the feminine form, emotion, and capturing what you love about yourself. They are inherently sexy because you are!

The photos have to be a gift

Did you know that you don't have to tell anyone about your boudoir photoshoot? It can be our cheeky secret, a beautiful gift for your someone special, or a reminder of just how beautiful you are. Your photos can be private or shared, the power to choose is yours!

You have to wear lingerie...or less

One of my favorite things about boudoir photography is how a woman's essence can shine through in sweatpants, a cocktail dress, or lingerie. During our pre-shoot consult, we will discuss your comfort level and I will never push you to reveal more than you feel comfortable with. Who knows, on the day of your photoshoot you might surprise yourself!

Only "perfect" bodies can participate

I've heard it all and truly, you don't need to change anything about yourself. You don't need to lose 10 more pounds. You don't need a tan. You don't need to be younger. It is my job to flatter you with posing and lighting, and believe me, it won't be hard to do!

You have to wear something expensive

Honestly, the only thing you need to wear is your personality. The outfits you choose should make you feel confident, so there's no need to save for a luxury brand. If an old t-shirt and boots is what brings out your inner goddess, then bring em!

A boudoir photoshoot is selfish
