Bonjour

Capturing the heartfelt and intimate moments that grace a woman’s life is what my brand is all about. From the gentle nurturing of children, to the serene embrace of a tranquil morning in bed with a pet, to the sacred and cherished bonds formed in a personalized elopement, or rediscovering your sensual essence with a glamorous boudoir session. Each experience is crafted with the utmost care, reverence, and infused with a touch of that French je ne sais quoi.

At the heart of what I do, is a love of empowering women through boudoir photography. I offer a sacred space where you can tune out the world that demands women do it all, degrades us when we show up, and drives us to the point of madness. Here, you will be accepted and celebrated. We will focus on what you love and what makes you unique. Your photos will emanate a French allure, evoking passion, elegance, and a profound sense of self-acceptance.

The Experience Step by Step

Collaborating on Your Journey

It all begins with an initial consultation where we connect on a personal level. Don't be shy, slide into my DMs! During this stage, we dive into your desires and vision, discussing the type of session you're interested in, exploring locations, and brainstorming styling options. I'll take the time to craft a custom session and quote specifically tailored to you. We'll also have a short phone call to ensure we're the perfect fit for each other.

Creating an Atmosphere of Trust and Confidence

Intimate boudoir portraits require a special level of trust, and I'm here to cultivate that with you. Your comfort is my priority, and I want you to feel empowered and beautiful throughout the entire experience. Before the session, we'll have conversations where I listen attentively to your concerns and anxieties, making sure we address them together. I have worked with diverse individuals, respecting and celebrating all body types, backgrounds, and comfort levels. Rest assured that I'll guide you with care, making sure you feel confident and empowered during your session. Our collaboration is key, and your involvement in the creative decision-making process is highly encouraged. We'll create mood boards, discuss styling choices, and have open lines of communication to ensure that your boudoir portraits are a true reflection of your unique personality and desires.

Image Selection and Retouching

I will reach out to you to schedule an ordering appointment, where we can share the excitement of selecting your images. Whether we meet in person or connect over Zoom, this will be a special moment to appreciate the artistry we've created together. We'll discuss various options for prints, albums, paintings, and wall art, ensuring that your final products are customized to your preferences. Rest assured, your images will always be handled securely and will never be shared without your permission.

The Final Reveal

The final reveal marks the culmination of your boudoir journey, and it's a moment that holds great significance to me. I'm dedicated to making it a memorable and uplifting experience for you. My ultimate goal is to create a final product that you will cherish for a lifetime—a tangible reminder of your strength, beauty, and the transformative journey of self-discovery you embarked upon. With each image, I aim to capture the essence of your unique spirit and empower you to embrace your inner beauty. Together, we'll celebrate your journey, creating a legacy that reminds you of your worth and radiance.
